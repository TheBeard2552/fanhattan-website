# Maya Chen

Maya Chen is a renowned hacker who operates from the shadows of the Neon District. Born into poverty, she taught herself programming by salvaging old terminals from the District's electronic waste heaps.

## Background

Growing up in the lower levels of Fanhattan, Maya witnessed firsthand the corruption and inequality perpetuated by the ruling corporate syndicates. Her natural aptitude for technology became her weapon of choice in the fight for justice.

## Abilities

- **Neural Interface Mastery**: Can directly interface with the city's mainframe
- **Ghost Protocol**: Ability to move through digital systems undetected
- **Code Breaking**: Expert in decrypting even the most secure systems

## Current Mission

Maya is currently investigating the mysterious Quantum Key, an artifact rumored to grant access to the city's core systems. She believes it holds the key to exposing the truth about Fanhattan's founders and their hidden agenda.
