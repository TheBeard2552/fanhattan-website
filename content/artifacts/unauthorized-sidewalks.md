## What It Is

Sidewalks that should not exist. Concrete laid where zoning forbids it, forming clean paths that all point toward the same destination.

## What It Means

Either the city is generating infrastructure the way other places generate weather, or someone learned how to fake a miracle. Either answer is bad.
