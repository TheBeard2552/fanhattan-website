# The Syndicate Offensive

An ongoing campaign where the Syndicate systematically targets neutral zones and independent districts. This thread represents the escalating tension between organized power and grassroots resistance.

## Current Status

The offensive is in full swing, with multiple districts feeling pressure. Some have already capitulated, while others are mounting resistance efforts.

## Key Players

- The Syndicate: Coordinating the offensive
- The Resistance: Organizing counter-efforts
- Independent district leaders: Forced to choose sides
