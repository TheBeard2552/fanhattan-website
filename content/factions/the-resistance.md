# The Resistance

The Resistance is a grassroots network of individuals and groups who oppose the Syndicate's consolidation of power. Unlike the Syndicate, they have no central leadership and often disagree on tactics.

## Philosophy

The Resistance believes in district autonomy and individual freedom. They see the Syndicate as a threat to the natural order of Fanhattan.

## Challenges

- Lack of coordination
- Limited resources
- Internal disagreements about methods
- Infiltration by Syndicate agents

## Strengths

- Local knowledge and support
- Moral high ground
- Adaptability and unpredictability
