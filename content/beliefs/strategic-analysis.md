# Strategic Analysis

The belief that careful planning and intelligence gathering beat improvisation. Those who hold this belief see the world as a chess game where information and forethought win.

## Core Tenets

- **Information Is Ammunition**: Know more than your opponent
- **Think Three Steps Ahead**: Anticipate consequences
- **Support Beats Solo Action**: The strategist enables the operator

## Application

Characters with this belief often work behind the scenes, providing the research and planning that makes frontline operations possible.
